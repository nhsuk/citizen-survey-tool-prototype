Tell the NHS about your current experience of coronavirus

This survey will help the NHS understand more about how and where coronavirus (COVID-19) is currently affecting people.

By answering some quick questions, you can help the NHS plan its response to coronavirus.

Important information:

If you need medical advice about your coronavirus symptoms, contact:

  • England: NHS 111 online coronavirus service
  • Scotland: NHS inform
  • Wales: NHS Direct Wales
  • Northern Ireland: Public Health Agency

We will not share the answers you give us beyond the NHS and trusted organisations working directly with the NHS in response to coronavirus.
