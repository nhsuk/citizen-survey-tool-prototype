Tell the NHS about your experience of coronavirus

This survey will help the NHS understand more about how and where coronavirus (COVID-19) is affecting people.

By answering some quick questions, you can help the NHS prepare and respond to coronavirus.

Important information:

If you need medical advice about your coronavirus symptoms, contact:

  • England: NHS 111 online coronavirus service
  • Scotland: NHS inform
  • Wales: NHS Direct Wales
  • Northern Ireland: Call 111

We will not share the answers you give us beyond the NHS and trusted organisations working directly with the NHS in response to coronavirus (COVID-19).
